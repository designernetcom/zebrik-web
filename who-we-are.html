WHO WE ARE

Established in the western Indian city of Pune in the year 2016, ZEBRIK VENTURES has remained true to its belief that “we are the ladder of success for our clients”.

ZEBRIK VENTURES is led by an experienced team, with expertise in different areas, but complementing each other to benefit its clients.

Leveraging its network in the global financial and corporate world, Zebrik has partnered with a host of major Banks, NBFCs and many Private Investors in India, to provide its clients with a range of customised solutions:

In the first 3 years, Zebrik notched up business worth more than USD 27 million / INR 200 Crore - primarily from Retail clients.

Late-2019 onwards, the pooling of their combined resources and networking brought on board larger institutional clients from the Services, Manufacturing, Software, Retail and Infra sectors ... but the Corona pandemic forced us to go slow with our plans.

However, working through the pandemic lockdown situation, Zebrik has recently signed an agreement with a European DEBT Fund to represent them as their Official CONSULTANT - with a mandate to cover clients from across geographies.

This strategic tie-up will help Zebrik raise long-term, low-interest Debt, via the FDI/ECB route and Equity, for medium and large companies and entrepreneurs - through the lender’s international fund.

We have now restarted the process of evaluating projects from a few of our clients in India, and also with our associates in Bangladesh, GCC and African countries ... with an ambitious target of raising USD 180 million / INR 1500 Crores in Debt for our Institutional clients, by March 2022.

INTERNATIONAL PROJECT FUNDING PROGRAM

ZEBRIK Ventures is the Official Representative of a large Fund (LENDER) based in Europe - which offers long term debt, through their International Fund, to fund profitable businesses and projects globally.

All funds managed and disbursed under this fund are from legal, clear and non-criminal EUR sources, and loans are issued through Banks in Europe, to ensure clean and clear funding for Projects and Businesses that meet all requirements.
